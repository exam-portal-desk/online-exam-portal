Frequently Asked Questions
Q: I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

A: Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Q: My exam is not loading. What should I do?

A: Check your internet connection, clear browser cache, and ensure JavaScript is enabled. If the problem persists, contact support immediately.

Q: Can I pause an exam once started?

A: No, exams cannot be paused once started. Ensure you have sufficient time before beginning.

Q: How do I view my exam results?

A: Results are available in your dashboard after the exam is submitted and processed by your institution.

Troubleshooting Guide

Connection Issues
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Switch to a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
Browser Issues
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Enable JavaScript and cookies
  • Update your browser to latest version
  • Try incognito/private browsing mode
